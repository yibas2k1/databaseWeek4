Apollo 11 Space Mission

On the16thof July, 1969, the Apollo 11 spacecraft launched from
the Kennedy Space Center in Florida. Its mission was to go where no
human being had gone before-the moon! Thecrew consisted by Neil
Armstrong,Michael Collins, and Buzz Aldrin. The spacecraft landed on
the moon in the Sea of Tranquility, a balastic flood plain, on the 20thof
July, 1969. The moonwalk took place the following day. On 21stJuly,
1969, at precisely10:56 EDT, Commander Neil Armstrong emerged
from the Lunar Module and took his famous first step onto the moon's
surface. He declared,"That's one small step for man, one giant leap
for mankind." It was a monumental moment in human history!
